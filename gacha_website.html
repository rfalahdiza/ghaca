How it Works?

GACHA uses advanced sensors to monitor soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and atmospheric pressure. Our proprietary software then uses AI-powered data analysis to predict weather patterns and optimal watering schedules.

The system automatically adjusts watering based on real-time data and weather forecasts, ensuring your plants receive the exact amount of water they need when they need it. GACHA takes care of watering so you can focus on enjoying your garden.
